World's Best Lasagna

Description

A dish made from flat pasta, tomato sauce and minced meat. Your mouth will water when you eat this delicious treat!

Just don't eat too much of it - too much of a good thing, after all!

Ingredients

  • Durum wheat pasta lasagna sheets
  • Beef minced meat
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Some vegetables
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in pan.
  2. Add assorted vegetables to pan and fry until soft.
  3. Add minced meat and fry until cooked on outside.
  4. Add the tomato sauce.
  5. Season to taste.
  6. Add butter, flour and milk to make a roux.
  7. Preheat your oven to 200C, fan 180C or gas mark 6.
  8. Layer the minced meat mix, white sauce and lasagna sheets.
  9. Top with lots of cheese.
  10. Bake for 45 minutes.
  11. Serve and enjoy!
