OK Lamb Curry

Description

Lamb curry is a favourite in restaurants in East London.

This lamb curry recipe is OK. Won't set the world on fire. Also won't set your mouth on fire so don't worry about the picture!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Diced lamb
  • Masala
  • Tomato sauce
  • Some vegetables
  • Olive oil
  • Some chillies (but don't go overboard)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in pan.
  2. Add minced meat and fry until cooked on outside.
  3. Add to slow cooker.
  4. Add more oil to pan and cook the vegetables.
  5. Add the chillies and masala.
  6. Also add to slow cooker.
  7. Add tomato sauce to slow cooker.
  8. Cook on low heat for 6 hours.
  9. Serve with rice (or naan, if you must).
