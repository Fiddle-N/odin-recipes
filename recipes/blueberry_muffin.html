Not Amazing Blueberry Muffin

Description

A blueberry muffin for diabetics.

It contains bran. Bran is good for you.

But it has sugar, so is it really good for you?

Ingredients

  • Bran
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • An egg
  • Brown sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Blueberries
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200C, fan 180C or gas mark 6.
  2. Mix bran and milk.
  3. Mix egg and sugar and vanilla. Mix into bran and milk mix.
  4. Add flour and baking powder
  5. Put in the blueberries without crushing them.
  6. Place into a cake tin.
  7. Bake for 30 mins or until the cake is done.
